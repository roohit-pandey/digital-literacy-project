## Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

###  Do’s

* Maintain a professional and clean profile
* Use strong passwords and enable security settings
* Think before posting any content
* Verify information before sharing
* Respect others and communicate politely online

###  Don’ts

* Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
* Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
* Do not click on suspicious links
* Avoid engaging in cyberbullying
* Do not spread misinformation or fake news
