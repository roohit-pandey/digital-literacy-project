## Cybercrime Case Study: Phishing Attack

Phishing is a common type of cybercrime. Cybercrime is a problem. In a phishing attack the bad people try to steal our information.
They want our passwords, one time passwords and bank details. They pretend to be someone we trust.They usually send us emails or messages.
Sometimes they make websites that look real. A phishing attack usually starts when we get an email that looks like it is from our bank or a company we know. 
The email says we have to do something now. If we do not do it our account will be blocked. The email has a link that takes us to a website. 
The fake website looks like the real one.When we put in our login details or personal information the bad people get it.
They use it to do things. They like to target students and young people. This is because they might not check if the email is real or not.
The bad things that can happen because of phishing are big.We can lose money. Someone can steal our identity. 
They can get into our accounts. Phishing attacks use tricks to make us do something. 
They do not use computer tricks. This is why we need to be aware of phishing attacks. 
We need to know what to do so we do not fall for a phishing attack. Phishing attacks are a type of cybercrime. 
Cybercrime is something we should all be careful about.
