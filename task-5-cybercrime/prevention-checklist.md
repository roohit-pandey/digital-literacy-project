## Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

### Safety Tips

* Don't share your passwords or one-time passwords with anyone.

* Always check the website address before entering information.

* Be careful when clicking on links you don't know or that seem suspicious.

* Use different passwords for each account.

* Turn on two-factor authentication whenever you can.

* Keep your software and apps updated regularly.

* Don't use Wi-Fi for financial transactions.

* Always log out when using shared devices.

### UPI Safety Tips

* Check the receivers details before making a payment.

* Don't accept payment requests, from people you don't know.

### Report Cybercrime

* National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in

* Helpline Number: 1930
